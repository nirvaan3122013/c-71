Bandipur National Park

I had visited in 2020

Bandipur National Park, located on the Mysore-Ooty highway in Karnataka, is a part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve, and shares its borders with Nagarhole National Park in Karnataka, Mudumalai Wildlife Sanctuary in Tamil Nadu, and Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary in Kerala. The national park is one of the largest national parks in the country.
